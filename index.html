Meet the Founder

Jeffrey Camp, CFA

Morgan Stanley MD CFA Charterholder 30+ Years Experience University of Michigan

At 22, I was living in Tokyo covering Japan's emerging mobile telecoms stocks — learning to read markets in a language I came to master, in a country building infrastructure for a mobile market that didn't exist yet. That instinct for being early has defined everything since.

Three decades later, the pattern is the same. I've sat at the table through the dot-com boom, built and run hedge funds in London and New York, backed AI infrastructure companies alongside Nvidia and Oracle, and watched a company I seed-funded grow from zero to $45M in recurring revenue. Every cycle, every market, every asset class — always looking for what comes next before the consensus finds it.

JC Venture Capital is where that experience lives today — a platform for principal investment across venture, public markets, and real estate, and a community for the sophisticated co-investors and founders who want to be in the room where it happens.
